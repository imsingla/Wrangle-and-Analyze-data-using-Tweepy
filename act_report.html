Conclusion¶

  1. There is a postive strong corelation between retweets and favorites as expected.
  2. Number of retweets and favorites are increasing over time showing increase in popularity of twitter page.
  3. Numerator Ratings given in recent times are always more than 10 as compared to recent times where there was more general distribution of ratings from 1 to 20.
  4. doggo is the most owned dog category whereas puppo is the least owned dog category according to this dataset though such causation cannot be confidently taken as true.
  5. puppper on an average gets minimum ratings whereas for others it is approximately equal.
  6. puppo on an average has higher confidence levels for right prediction in dog categories.
